The ocellaris clownfish also known as the false percula clownfish or common clownfish, is a marine fish belonging to the family Pomacantridae which includes clownfishes and damselfishes
.Amphiprion ocellaris are found in different color, depending on where they are located.
For example, black amphiprion ocellaris with white bands can be found near northern Australia, Southeast Asia, and Japan.
Orange or red-brown AMphiprion ocellaris also exist with three similar white bands on the body and head.
Amphiprion ocellaris can be distunguished from other Amphiprion species based on the number of pectoral rays and dorsal spines.
